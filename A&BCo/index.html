About Us

The Artist and Baker Company is run by two members of a family. This company is based in Kozhikode,Kerala. Our family business is a unique collaboration between an artist and a baker, intertwining their individual passions and expertise to create something truly extraordinary. With a shared vision and a deep understanding of each other's craft, we have found the perfect synergy in our collaborative company. The artist's creative vision brings vibrant colors, expressive forms, and captivating designs to our delectable creations. The baker's mastery of flavors, textures, and techniques ensures that every bite is a delicious delight. Together, we seamlessly blend artistry and baking, infusing our creations with a touch of magic and a feast for the senses. As a family, we are united by our shared love for art and baking, and our commitment to delivering exceptional products. With each creation, we honor the traditions and values that have been passed down through generations, while embracing innovation and pushing the boundaries of our craft. When you experience our collaborative company, you will be captivated by the artistic expressions and mouthwatering flavors that emerge from our family's shared passion and creativity. Welcome to a world where art and baking converge, guided by the love and unity that defines our family.
